Kebab

Kebab is tende type of kebab and is made using ground beef or lamb. They are formed into skewers.


Ingredients

  • 450g ground meat
  • 30g ground beef or lamb fat
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
    • <1i>1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • fresh parsley
  • fresh cilanto

Instructions
  1. Mix all the ingredients together.
  2. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  3. Shape the mixture into sausage shapes on a skewer.
  4. Cook them for approximately 5 minutes.
